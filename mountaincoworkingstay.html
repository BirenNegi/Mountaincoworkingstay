Our Story

A place where
deep work meets
wild mountains.

Tucked in the oak and rhododendron forests of Sarainkhet, Almora at 1800 metres, mountaincoworkingstay.com was built for one kind of person: the remote worker who knows that their best thinking happens when they're free.

Free from the city. Free from the commute. Free from the grey sameness of a co-working café. Here, the office view is the Himalayas, the commute is a forest trail, and the afternoon break is a bike ride to a panorama that stops you in your tracks.

Minimum 7 nights. We are selective because we believe the right community makes everything better — stay longer, think deeper, live fully.

Forest & Alpine
Weekend Camping

Every weekend, the mountains call and you answer. Trek to a forest clearing or an open alpine meadow. Set up camp. Cook over fire.
